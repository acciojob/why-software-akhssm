Why I want to become a developer?

I want to become a software developer because I enjoy solving problems and building things that can help people in their everyday lives. Technology is always evolving, and being a developer allows me to continuously learn and improve my skills while contributing to meaningful projects. My motivation comes from the excitement of turning ideas into real applications that can make a positive impact. I believe software development gives me the opportunity to express creativity, collaborate with others, and grow both personally and professionally.
